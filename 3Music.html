Current Projects

Absent Prayer

We are a band based in Toronto, Ontario. We are at the moment doing all covers. You will find the band's taste to be more mainstream. We try to record some music every month. We got together in mid 2018. Our goal is to regularly perform local shows.

CtrlC CtrlV

We are a band based in Toronto, Ontario. We are at the moment doing all original songs. The songs are for a concept album. This will be an interesting project as the concept is very funny but we are trying to incorporate different style of music for our songs. We officially got together in the start of 2018. Our goal is to have fun and make some music.

Shadow in Flames

We are a band based in Cambridge, Ontario. We haven't delved into originals yet. Normally, we do covers of classic rock songs. We got together in the end of 2017. Our goal is to do some local shows and delve into originals.

Solo Project

My solo project is a concept album of 5 songs. It emcompasses lot of different themes: revenge, murder, supernatural, remorse, etc. The story will be evident when listening to the tracks. I am the sole producer for this album. My goal is to have fun and learn recording and writing skills in the process.

Previous Projects

Frosh Week Band

Every year I became a Orientation Leader for University of Waterloo. On the 5th day of the orientation week there would be a band performance in the background while the EDCOM put on a different show for all first year engineering students. I was an orientation leader three times and every year I was the guitarist for the band.

Oreo

This was my band in my high school. The members were Eric Ouellet, Sean Thomas Maraj and I. I am still in touch with them both. We used to chill and try to do some rock songs. Most of the time was just us hanging out.

12 Eyes

This was another band of mine in my high school. The members were Jasmeet Oberoi, Sean Thomas Maraj and I. I am still in touch with them both. We used to chill and try to do some fusion of hard rock, classic rock and Indian music.

Miscellaneous

Other than these, I have performed multiple times in high-school and university in various cultural shows and events. I have been invited by other universities and organisations to perform in their events as well.
